Physical objects catch light. Digital cards usually do not. That gap is exactly why a well-implemented reflection effect feels so satisfying when you first encounter it.

## What it does

The Reflective Card component simulates a moving specular highlight on a card surface. As you move the cursor across the card, a bright sheen tracks your pointer angle, making the card feel like it has a physical finish.

## How it works

The component tracks mouse position relative to the card and calculates an angle from the center. That angle maps to the position of a semi-transparent linear gradient overlay layered above the card content.

The gradient transitions smoothly using CSS transitions on the background-position, so the light always feels like it is gliding rather than jumping.

## Key properties

- **children** - Card content
- **reflectionColor** - Color of the light sheen (default: white)
- **reflectionOpacity** - Maximum opacity of the overlay (default: 0.15)
- **borderRadius** - Corner radius of the card

## When to use it

This works beautifully on product cards, profile cards, or any card where you want to reinforce a premium feel. The effect is most readable on cards with a solid, mid-tone background rather than a busy image.

Less is more. A subtle reflection at 10-20% opacity looks polished. Going higher starts to look like a UI bug.
